Sinigang

Sinigang is a sour and savory Filipino soup or stew. The word "sinigang" comes from the Tagalog word meaning "stewed"

Ingredients

  • 1 kg of Pork
  • 1 Sinigang mix
  • 1 Pork cube
  • 1 Onion
  • 3 Tomatoes
  • Spinach or Kangkong (300 grams)
  • String beans (Depends on how much you like to put on)
  • taro(if you want)
